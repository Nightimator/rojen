dream Diner

It's night time. You're driving but it's raining heavily. You see a Diner nearby with the lights on...


You died.

Your car broke down and you froze to death in the snow.






You Survived.

You leave your car and enter the Diner...





A waiter takes you to your table, but he has no face and only a gaint mouth full of sharp teeth. Everyone else sitting on the Diner has black eyes. They all turn to look at you...


Rules:

  1. 1. Don't drink the water. There's something inside it that you can't see.
  2. 2. Don't order off the Menu.
  3. 3. Don't get caught in the kitchen. If you see what they're REALLY cooking, they will kill you.
  4. 4. If they hear your voice, they will know you don't belong.
  5. 5. Find a way to escape.



The waiter puts a jug of water on the table. What do you do?


You Died.

The bugs inside the water ate your throat






You Survived.

For now...






You Died.

The waiter dragged you into the kitchen and boiled you alive





As you're looking at the menu, a girl from the table behind you stands up and walks towards you. You see her eyes are normal. She hands you a note...


You Died.

They heard you. They know you don't belong so they cut all your limbs off and hung them up with meat hooks...






You Survived...






You Died.

The girl starts screaming. The waiter ties you and the girl together and shoves you in the oven to cook you...





The note says:
"Front door is a trap. Escape is in the kitchen." The girl points to the kitchen, hinting that you should follow her inside. Do you trust her?


You Died.

but you're drawing the attention. The other guests are staring at you both...

Now you must escape together...
